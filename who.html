## Who Celebrates Halloween? Halloween is celebrated by people of all ages, but the way it's celebrated can vary depending on age groups and cultural backgrounds: - **Children:** Often the most enthusiastic participants, engaging in trick-or-treating, costume contests at school, and other Halloween-themed activities. - **Teenagers and Young Adults:** Typically enjoy costume parties, haunted attractions, and horror movie marathons. - **Adults:** May participate in costume parties, decorate their homes, hand out candy to trick-or-treaters, or attend Halloween-themed events. - **Families:** Often celebrate together, with parents accompanying children for trick-or-treating or organizing family-friendly Halloween activities. - **Communities:** Many towns and cities organize Halloween parades, festivals, or community events. It's worth noting that participation in Halloween celebrations can be influenced by factors such as religious beliefs, cultural background, and personal preferences. While it's widely celebrated in countries like the United States and Canada, not everyone in these countries necessarily participates in Halloween activities.
